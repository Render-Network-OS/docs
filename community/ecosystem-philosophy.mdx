## Pillars

- **Sustainability:** Long-term ecosystem health and treasury planning
- **Aligned Incentives:** Creators, viewers, and traders benefit together
- **Gamification:** Engagement loops that reward participation
- **Creator Control:** Ownership over content and monetization paths
- **Crypto-Native Tools:** Solana-first architecture for transparency

## Execution

- Token-gated tools for creators
- Transparent fee flows and reporting
- Community-driven roadmaps
