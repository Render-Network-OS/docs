## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I earn USDC?" icon="circle-dollar-to-slot">
    Play games on the 555 Arcade to earn points throughout the week. Points are converted to USDC rewards during the weekly settlement cycle. Settlement occurs every **Monday at 05:55 CST** — after settlement, you can claim your USDC via the CTRL Panel claims page.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What wallets are supported?" icon="wallet">
    **Phantom** is the recommended wallet for the best experience. **Solflare** and **Backpack** are also supported. Any Solana-compatible wallet that supports standard transaction signing should work, but Phantom receives the most testing and support.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I report a bug?" icon="bug">
    Report bugs through the Discord **#support** channel for fastest response, or email **support@rndrntwrk.com** with a detailed description of the issue, screenshots, your wallet address, and the browser/OS you are using.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="When are payouts?" icon="calendar-check">
    Payouts are processed every **Monday at 05:55 CST**. After settlement, rewards become claimable via Merkle proof on the CTRL Panel. Allow up to 1 hour after the scheduled time for the distribution tree to be published.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need $555 to play?" icon="coins">
    No — all games on the 555 Arcade are free to play. Holding $555 tokens unlocks **multipliers** that increase your point earnings and provides access to enhanced tiers with additional benefits. See [Subscriptions & Staking](/products/subscriptions-staking) for tier details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I disconnect my wallet mid-game?" icon="plug-circle-xmark">
    Your current game session will end and any unsaved score from that round may be lost. Reconnect your wallet and start a new game round. Previously submitted scores are not affected.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I play from mobile?" icon="mobile">
    The platform is optimized for desktop browsers. Mobile support is limited — some games may work on mobile browsers with wallet extensions, but the experience is not guaranteed. A dedicated mobile experience is on the roadmap.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I check my reward history?" icon="clock-rotate-left">
    Navigate to the CTRL Panel and view the **Rewards** section. You can see your earning history, pending claims, and past settlement details. All settlement transactions are also verifiable on-chain via a Solana block explorer.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
