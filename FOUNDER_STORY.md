# RNDRNTWRK — Founder Story & Vision

I found a multi‑billion‑dollar market waiting to be taken: dynamic ads on live streams, done right. Today, most creators pin a static badge or hack overlays by hand. Advertisers who want the long tail can’t find, target, or trust what they’re buying. The result is under‑monetized creators and frustrated brands.

RNDRNTWRK is my answer. I’m building a Solana‑native, real‑time overlay system and ad marketplace that makes dynamic, non‑intrusive placements simple, measurable, and aligned with creators.

## The Opportunity

- Live attention is massive, but tooling is pre‑social: static, manual, brittle. Brands want targeted reach with safety; creators want reliable income without selling their soul.
- No one has packaged dynamic overlays + a buy‑side experience that works across many streams at once, with creator control and real‑time performance.
- This is bigger than sponsorships. It’s a streaming ad network—composable, contextual, and moment‑based.

## The Wedge (MVP “555”)

- Step 1: Normalize overlays with something creators love using. That’s “555”—high‑retention arcade games, reactions, and rewards that make streams more fun. It grows inventory (places we can later render ads) while building the muscle memory of using overlays.
- Step 2: Make overlays effortless to add to OBS, Streamlabs, and any platform (Twitch, Kick, YouTube). One control panel, all destinations.
- Step 3: Ship this to early creators in crypto‑native livestreams (Pump.fun, Binance Square, Zora) to battle‑harden the system, then expand everywhere.

## The Plan (Pragmatic Roadmap)

1) Create the overlay MVP that will hold ads in the future
2) Simplify integration with OBS/Streamlabs and multi‑stream workflows
3) Launch 555 to incentivize adoption and prove engagement lift
4) Monetize: dynamic ad placements across many streams; sponsor‑specific campaigns when needed
   - Tailored creatives, 50/50 split between platform and participating creators
   - Google‑Ads‑style delivery: many overlays, controlled targeting, brand safety
5) Scale and optimize: better matching, measurement, creative formats, and tools for both sides

## Why Blockchain (and Why Solana)

- Real‑time, micro‑settlement rails for revenue sharing (fair, transparent splits)
- Wallet‑based identity and gating for creator features and advertiser access
- On‑chain telemetry for verifiable delivery and payouts (where sensible)
- Solana is built for low‑latency, high‑throughput interactions that map to streaming’s real‑time needs

## “Always‑On” Streaming

- Somewhere in the pipeline, we run streams for you when you sleep or step away. Incentives keep viewers engaged while overlays and monetization stay active. You don’t need your local OBS grinding 24/7.

## Go‑To‑Market (Organic First)

- Start with crypto‑native streams (Pump.fun / Binance Square / Zora) to harden the tech and build the first thousand users.
- Multi‑stream creators get the magic immediately: control once, apply everywhere.
- Expand to Twitch, Kick, YouTube with partnerships and creator success stories.

## Business Model

- Dynamic ad exchange across many streams with brand safety and creative control
- 50% platform / 50% creators on ad revenue; sponsor‑specific campaigns supported
- Non‑intrusive formats: subtle video passes, themed overlays, contextual triggers (e.g., chat bursts, achievements)

## Current Status

- One month into execution; building solo; hosting on my PC to conserve resources while shipping
- Overlay system, control panel, and gateway are live in MVP form
- 555 is the on‑ramp: proving retention and normalizing overlays

## What MetaDAO Enables

- Team: hire the core engineering, design, SRE, QA, community
- Infra: production‑grade reliability for high volume
- Network: warm intros to creators and brands to seed the marketplace
- Guidance: experienced governance and GTM mentorship to reach PMF

## Risk & Mitigation (Real Talk)

- Security: we’ve already published a production readiness audit; next we rotate secrets, migrate to HttpOnly cookies + CSRF, harden uploads/CSP/ratelimits, add migrations and observability. Budgeted and scheduled.
- Adoption: 555 reduces friction by being fun and useful even before monetization; it builds the habit we need for the ad layer to flourish.
- Platform dynamics: multi‑stream support and non‑intrusive formats lower the risk of platform‑specific policy changes.

## Why This Will Win

- We’re not retrofitting banner ads into streams. We’re building streaming‑native, real‑time placements that creators actually want to run and brands can trust. With the right team and runway, we can capture a quarter of a very large market.
