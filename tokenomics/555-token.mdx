- 10% of all revenue → ARP (Audience Reward Pool) — off the top
- Post-ARP: 50% creator / 50% platform (configurable)
- Platform's share: 70% Treasury, 20% buyback-and-burn, 5% $555 reserve, 5% SOL/USDC reserve
