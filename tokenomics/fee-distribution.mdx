### Creator & Streamer Rewards

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Direct Payout System" icon="zap">
    **Instant Reward Distribution:**

    **Payout Mechanics:**
    - **Processing Time:** &lt; 5 seconds after transaction
    - **Fee Structure:** 0% platform fee on payouts
    - **Supported Currencies:** SOL, USDC, $555
    - **Minimum Payout:** Asset-specific threshold (anti-dust protection)

    **Payout Methods:**
    - **Wallet Direct:** Instant transfer to connected wallet
    - **Exchange Integration:** Direct to exchange accounts
    - **Bank Transfer:** Fiat conversion available
    - **Gift Cards:** Digital reward options

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Performance Bonuses" icon="trophy">
    **Achievement-Based Rewards:**

    **Bonus Categories:**
    - **Engagement Bonus:** 10% extra for high viewer interaction
    - **Loyalty Bonus:** 5% extra for returning viewers
    - **Quality Bonus:** 15% for high-quality content
    - **Growth Bonus:** 20% for channel growth milestones

    **Calculation Formula:**
    ```
    Total Reward = Base Fee × Creator Share × Performance Multiplier
    Performance Multiplier = 1.0 + (Engagement × 0.1) + (Quality × 0.15) + (Growth × 0.2)
    ```

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Tiered Reward System" icon="layers">
    **Creator Progression Tiers:**

    **Tier Structure:**
    - **Bronze:** 0-100 monthly earnings (standard 70%)
    - **Silver:** 100-500 monthly earnings (75% share)
    - **Gold:** 500-2000 monthly earnings (80% share)
    - **Platinum:** 2000+ monthly earnings (85% share)

    **Tier Benefits:**
    - **Priority Support:** Faster response times
    - **Advanced Analytics:** Detailed performance insights
    - **Custom Branding:** White-label options
    - **Revenue Optimization:** AI-powered suggestions

  </Accordion>
