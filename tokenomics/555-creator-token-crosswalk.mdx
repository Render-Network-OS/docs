## Roles & Relationship

- <strong>$555:</strong> Platform token for access, staking into creator economies, and network-level coordination
- <strong>Creator Token:</strong> Per-creator economy launched with Render Expand; used for staking, governance, and buybacks

## Staking Flow

1) Viewers stake into a creator’s economy (using $555 or the Creator Token as configured)
2) Stakeholders earn from the 70% Profit Pool and gain voting rights for creator/stakeholder split within bounds
3) Unstake with per-creator cooldown windows

## Buybacks (≥20%)

- A minimum of 20% of revenue attributable to a creator is used for automated buybacks of that creator’s token
- Creators may increase this to accelerate appreciation (subject to governance)
