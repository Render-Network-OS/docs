## The $555 Economy

RNDRNTWRK’s tokenomics are not a bolt-on incentive wrapper. They are part of the operating logic of the network: access, staking, participation, treasury growth, and structural buybacks all sit inside one system. The economy centers on $555 and a creator-specific staking model that turns participants into stakeholders.

<Info>
  **Token:** $555 (SPL, Solana) · **Contract:** `CQwwRomsuWsUCPYomZmRnwMns4ZCTASc31ExMvSysAF2` · **Total Supply:** 1,000,000,000 $555 · **Public:** 92% · **Team:** 8% (locked 5 years) · **Core Mechanism:** Algorithmic split of all revenues into 10% ARP, 20%+ Token Buybacks, and 70% Treasury · **Stakeholder Model:** Viewers stake into creator economies to share revenue and govern the split
</Info>

## Revenue Distribution — Two-Level Cascade

The same revenue pattern applies at **two levels** across the ecosystem. At both levels, revenue is processed identically:

<Info>
  **The Canonical Cascade (applies at both levels):** **10% ARP** off the top for verified audience members via VAP attention proofs. **Post-ARP Split:** The remaining 90% splits between Creator and Platform (configurable). **Platform Allocation:** 70% Treasury (operations), 20% token buyback-and-burn, 5% token reserve, 5% SOL/USDC reserve.
</Info>

### Level 1: Platform Level ($555 as the "Creator")

<Info>
  At the platform level, $555 acts as the "creator" in the cascade. All gross revenue first has **10% ARP** taken off the top for audience rewards. The remaining 90% splits **50/50 between the $555 ecosystem (creator) and the platform**. The platform's 50% is allocated: 70% treasury operations, 20% $555 buyback-and-burn, 5% $555 reserve, 5% SOL/USDC reserve.
</Info>

### Level 2: Creator Economy Level (Individual Creator Tokens)

<Info>
  At the creator economy level, individual creator tokens follow the same cascade. **10% ARP** off the top for audience rewards, then a configurable creator/platform split on the remaining 90%. Treasury, buybacks, and reserves follow the same pattern within each creator's economy.
</Info>
