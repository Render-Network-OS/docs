## Revenue Distribution — Two-Level Cascade

The same revenue pattern applies at **two levels** across the ecosystem. At both levels, revenue is processed identically:

<Info>
  <strong>The Canonical Cascade (applies at both levels):</strong>
  <br />
  <strong>10% – ARP (Audience Reward Pool):</strong> Off the top — goes directly to verified audience members via VAP attention proofs.
  <br />
  <strong>Post-ARP Split:</strong> The remaining 90% splits between Creator and Platform (configurable).
  <br />
  <strong>Platform Allocation:</strong> 70% Treasury (operations), 20% token buyback-and-burn, 5% token reserve, 5% SOL/USDC reserve.
</Info>

### Level 1: Platform Level ($555 as the "Creator")

<Info>
  At the platform level, $555 acts as the "creator" in the cascade. All gross revenue first has <strong>10% ARP</strong> taken off the top for audience rewards. The remaining 90% splits <strong>50/50 between the $555 ecosystem (creator) and the platform</strong>. The platform's 50% is allocated: 70% treasury operations, 20% $555 buyback-and-burn, 5% $555 reserve, 5% SOL/USDC reserve.
</Info>

### Level 2: Creator Economy Level (Individual Creator Tokens)

<Info>
  At the creator economy level, individual creator tokens follow the same cascade. <strong>10% ARP</strong> off the top for audience rewards, then a configurable creator/platform split on the remaining 90%. Treasury, buybacks, and reserves follow the same pattern within each creator's economy.
</Info>
