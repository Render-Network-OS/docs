## RNDR Airdrop Program

Comprehensive airdrop system designed to reward early adopters, community contributors, and active participants in the Render Network ecosystem.

<Info>
  **Total Airdrop Pool:** 400M RNDR (40% of total supply) **Distribution
  Method:** Multi-phase claiming system **Claim Period:** 6 months post-TGE
  **Eligibility:** Multi-factor verification required
</Info>
