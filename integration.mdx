<Info>
  Keep it simple. Follow these steps to get live fast — screenshots coming soon.

  Set up in the following steps
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to CTRL">
    Go directly to CTRL Panel: https://rendernet.work/ctrl-panel
    
    Access requires 5,000,000 $555. If you don’t meet the threshold, request whitelist access via Discord.
    
    ![CTRL Panel (no wallet connected)](https://res.cloudinary.com/davqktkik/image/upload/v1761227703/ctrl-no-wallet_hnwsy2.png)
    
    ![CTRL Panel (not enough tokens)](https://res.cloudinary.com/davqktkik/image/upload/v1761227701/ctrl-not-enough-tokens_l5hlch.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect your wallet">
    In CTRL, click Connect and authenticate with your Solana wallet (e.g., Phantom or Solflare).
    
    ![Connect Wallet](https://res.cloudinary.com/davqktkik/image/upload/v1761227702/connect-wallet_vrvhku.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy your overlay link">
    In CTRL, copy your overlay URL for your current profile/theme.
    
    ![Copy Overlay Link](https://res.cloudinary.com/davqktkik/image/upload/v1761227702/ctrl-panel-copy-link_rijqom.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add games, alerts, and reactions">
    In CTRL, enable the modules you want (games, alerts, reactions) before adding the overlay to OBS.
    
    ![Enable Modules (games, alerts, reactions)](https://res.cloudinary.com/davqktkik/image/upload/v1761227700/ctrl-modules_kq6dzg.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open OBS Studio">
    Launch OBS.
    
    ![OBS: Add Source](https://res.cloudinary.com/davqktkik/image/upload/v1761227702/obs-add-source_muv0nu.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add a Browser Source">
    In the Sources panel, click the + icon → Browser Source.
    
    ![OBS: Select Browser Source](https://res.cloudinary.com/davqktkik/image/upload/v1761227700/obs-select-browser-source_awu02n.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Paste your overlay URL">
    Paste the URL you copied from CTRL and set the resolution to 1920 × 1080. Click OK.
    
    ![OBS: Name Source](https://res.cloudinary.com/davqktkik/image/upload/v1761227701/obs-source-name_klsc7o.png)
    
    ![OBS: URL + Width/Height](https://res.cloudinary.com/davqktkik/image/upload/v1761227700/obs-url-width-height-set_bm7xqe.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete">
    You’re done. Go live and verify events render over your scene.
    
    ![OBS: Complete](https://res.cloudinary.com/davqktkik/image/upload/v1761227700/obs-complete_j1t4k6.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
