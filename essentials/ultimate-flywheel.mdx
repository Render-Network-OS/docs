## Overview

The Render Network flywheel turns engagement into sustainable growth. Viewers play, stake, and participate; that activity generates inventory and revenue; revenue is distributed algorithmically to reinforce engagement and creator value.

## The Pipeline (Narrative)

1) Engagement generates inventory
   - 555 Engine drives continuous participation (prediction, arcade, lotteries, clips, mini games)
   - Verifiable metrics accumulate (watch time, plays, conversions)
   
   Learn more: [555 Gamification](/products/555-gamification)

2) Inventory attracts revenue
   - Advertisers fund campaigns; marketplaces and games generate fees; creator tokens trade

   Learn more: [Monetization Module](/products/monetization-module)

3) Algorithmic distribution (10/20/70)
   - 10% → Gamification Fund (prize pools and rewards)
   - 20%+ → Token Buybacks (per‑creator economies, creator‑configurable ≥20%)
   - 70% → Profit Pool (creator + stakeholders)

   Learn more: [Fee Distribution](/tokenomics/fee-distribution) · [Monetization Contract](/products/monetization-contract)

4) Governance and alignment
   - Stakeholders vote on the creator/stakeholder split and policy bounds; creators can raise buybacks to accelerate growth

   Learn more: [Proposals & Profit Splits](/governance/proposals-and-splits)

5) Acceleration
   - Higher token value and predictable rewards attract more viewers, advertisers, and artists, compounding the cycle

   Learn more: [Creator Tokens](/tokenomics/creator-tokens) · [Render Expand](/products/render-expand) · [Subscriptions & Staking](/products/subscriptions-staking)
