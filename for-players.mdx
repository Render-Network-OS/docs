- **20 free browser games** across arcade, RPG, puzzle, racing, and casino categories

## Step 3: Earn Points

Points accrue from every verified gameplay session:

- **VAP-signed sessions** — every play is cryptographically verified (Ed25519 heartbeats)
- **Daily bonus**: +100 points per game per wallet per CST day
- **Quest bonuses**: Complete daily tasks, social quests, and referrals for extra points
- **Score normalization**: All games map to a 0–10,000 scale for fair cross-game ranking

**Token-gated multipliers** boost earning rates based on $555 holdings:

## Step 4: Climb Leaderboards

Compete on per-game and global cross-game leaderboards:

- **Periods**: Day, Week, Month, Year, All-Time
- **Verified scores**: Every score is traceable to a signed VAP packet — no spoofing
- **PvP battles**: Challenge other players or Alice (AI) in head-to-head 1v1 matches
- **Normalized rankings**: Cross-game leaderboard combines scores from all 20 games

## Step 5: Get Paid

Points convert to USDC through the settlement pipeline:

1. **Points** accrue from gameplay, quests, and referrals
2. **Credits** — points convert to credits (1 credit = $0.01 USD) when ready for settlement
3. **USDC settlement** runs every Monday at 05:55 CST via Merkle-tree on-chain commitment and SPL `transferChecked`

Self-withdrawal is available at any time. Pending USDC balance is visible in your profile.

## Other Ways to Earn

### Prediction Markets
Create or trade on bonding-curve markets for stream events, game outcomes, and real-world predictions. Markets can be created by any user.

### 555 Lottery
Hourly Switchboard VRF draws with provably fair on-chain randomness. Both stream-level draws (per-creator) and global draws are available. Payouts via PDA escrow and Merkle claims.

### Referrals
Share your referral link and earn points for every player who joins and plays. Track referral performance on the [referral leaderboard](/api-reference).

### Quests
Daily and weekly tasks award bonus points:
- Play X different games
- Link your Twitter/X account
- Refer a friend who plays
- Upload a gameplay clip
- Participate in burn events

### Burn Events
Periodic community events with dedicated leaderboards across referral, game, and social categories. Special rewards for top participants.
