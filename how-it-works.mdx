## The Market Loop

RNDRNTWRK runs one continuous economic cycle. Creation becomes inventory, inventory attracts value, and value routes algorithmically back to every participant:

### 1. Engagement Generates Inventory

Players play games, viewers watch streams, and participants interact with the ecosystem. The **Verifiable Attention Protocol (VAP)** verifies each session with Ed25519 cryptographic heartbeats every 5 seconds. Verified engagement becomes **Proof of Engagement** — the atomic unit of value in the protocol.

### 2. Inventory Attracts Revenue

Verified attention creates inventory that advertisers, marketplaces, and games monetize:
- **L-Bar Ads**: Non-interruptive squeeze-back ads placed by AI at optimal engagement moments
- **Prediction Markets**: Bonding-curve AMM markets on stream events, game outcomes, and real-world predictions
- **Game Fees**: Entry fees, tournament prizes, and casino/lottery pools
- **Creator Token Trading**: Per-creator SPL tokens with enforced economic splits

### 3. Revenue Is Distributed Algorithmically

Every dollar follows the same cascade. No manual payouts, no discretionary allocation.

| Step | Allocation | Recipient |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. ARP** | 10% off the top | Audience Reward Pool — distributed to verified engaged audience members |
| **2. Creator** | 50% of post-ARP | Creator's wallet |
| **3. Platform** | 50% of post-ARP | Platform allocation (see below) |

**Platform allocation breakdown:**

| Share | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- |
| 70% | Treasury — operations, development, growth |
| 20% | $555 buyback-and-burn — structural deflation |
| 5% | $555 token reserve |
| 5% | SOL/USDC reserve |

### 4. Distribution Accelerates Growth

- Audience rewards attract more players (ARP pays verified participants)
- Token burns increase $555 scarcity (20% of platform revenue, permanently)
- Creator economies compound (buybacks reinforce creator token value)
- Higher engagement → more inventory → more revenue → repeat

## Points & Credits

The protocol uses a two-layer reward system that converts engagement into USDC.

**Points** are the engagement reward:
- **Conversion**: 10,000 points = 1 USDC (fixed)
- Non-transferable accounting abstraction
- Earned through gameplay, quests, referrals, and engagement
- Daily bonus: +100 points per game per wallet per CST day

**Credits** are the settlement unit:
- 1 credit = $0.01 USD
- Append-only ledger with 1.25x burn bonus for $555 burns
- Points convert to credits when ready for settlement

**Weekly USDC settlement** runs every Monday at 05:55 CST via Merkle-tree on-chain commitment and SPL `transferChecked`. Self-withdrawal is available at any time.
