## Run Media as an Economy

Render Network Protocol gives creators a native economic system. Distribute from your browser, activate participation loops across games, clips, quests, and predictions, and route value through one transparent structure instead of stitching together disconnected platforms and tools.

## What You Get

- **Browser-native distribution** — broadcast everywhere all at once via custom RTMP output, no download required
- **Non-interruptive L-Bar ads** with AI-optimized placement that never interrupt content
- **20 arcade games** embedded in your stream for audience participation and economic activity
- **Points/credits economy** that pays your audience in USDC weekly
- **Creator Token** with buyback-and-burn mechanics for your own economic surface
- **Alice** (AI operator) that can run your stream autonomously 24/7 — or run it with your team, the system serves both

## Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

Connect a Solana wallet to access the protocol:

- **Phantom** (recommended), Solflare, or Trust Wallet
- Authentication via **SIWS** (Sign In With Solana) — sign once per session
- Access requirement: **55,555 $555** minimum (or request whitelist access via [Discord](https://discord.gg/35UNPtu8M5))

## Step 2: Open 555stream

Open [stream.rndrntwrk.com](https://stream.rndrntwrk.com) in your browser.

- No OBS, no desktop app, no download
- Cloud encoding at the edge — no GPU load on your machine
- Consistent stream quality regardless of local hardware

## Step 3: Configure Your Broadcast

Set up your stream destinations and scene:

- **Add RTMP keys** for each platform: Twitch, YouTube, Kick, X, TikTok, Zora, pump.fun, and any other RTMP-compatible destination
- **Choose a scene layout**: Solo, 2-grid, 4-grid, PIP (Picture-in-Picture)
- **Configure overlays**: lower thirds, tickers, alerts, branding, and sponsor assets
- **Invite guests**: WebRTC-based guest system with independent audio/video control

## Step 4: Enable Engagement Modules

Activate interactive features from the CTRL Panel to drive participation:

- **Games**: Enable [555 Arcade](/arcade/overview) games in your overlay — 20 browser games with leaderboards
- **Prediction Markets**: Let viewers create and trade on bonding-curve markets for stream events and game outcomes
- **Lottery**: Switchboard VRF-powered hourly draws with on-chain proofs and PDA escrow payouts
- **Quests**: Daily/weekly challenges tied to engagement goals (play X games, clip highlights, refer friends)
- **Clips**: Viewers clip highlights for points; top clips surface in your overlays

## Step 5: Monetize

Revenue flows through the standard [economic cascade](/how-it-works):

1. **10% ARP** to your engaged viewers automatically
2. **50% of post-ARP** to your wallet
3. **L-Bar ads**: Configure campaign acceptance, schedule, and frequency caps — ads never interrupt content
4. **Prediction market fees** and game entry fees contribute to your revenue

**Track earnings** in real-time from the studio dashboard. Weekly USDC settlement runs every Monday at 05:55 CST.

## Step 6: Launch a Creator Token (Optional)

<Info>**Status: Partially Implemented** — Creator Token infrastructure is being rolled out via [Render Expand](/products/render-expand).</Info>

Mint your own Creator Token via pump.fun with enforced economic splits:

- **Staker-funded revenue sharing** — your audience invests in your growth
- **Token-gated chat, overlays, and actions** — exclusive experiences for holders
- **Your own buyback-and-burn flywheel** — 20%+ of revenue buys and burns your token
- **Governance** — stakeholders vote on creator/stakeholder split and policy bounds

## Alice: Your AI Operator

[Alice](/alice/persona) is an autonomous AI agent that can operate your stream without human intervention:

- **Start/stop broadcasts** on schedule
- **Trigger L-Bar ads** at optimal engagement moments (5-second polling across 4 engagement signals, 0.70 threshold)
- **Manage guests** — invite, control audio/video, adjust grid layouts
- **Control overlays** — switch scenes, update tickers, trigger alerts
- **24/7 streaming**: Alice runs the broadcast; you join for structured segments

## Always-On Mode

Your channel stays active even when you're offline:

- **Games continue** — scheduled raids, hourly lottery, evergreen mini-games
- **Clips resurface** — past stream highlights (including YouTube Shorts, X, TikTok, Instagram clips) cycle in overlays
- **Auto Pilot** automates programming — schedules events and rotates experiences
- **Community stays engaged**, compounding growth and retention
