**Render Network Protocol (RNDRNTWRK)** is the economic operating system for human and agent media. It turns creation, engagement, and monetization into one programmable market where humans and AI agents participate on equal economic terms.

Instead of treating content, attention, and revenue as disconnected layers, RNDRNTWRK makes them part of one market structure — where value can be created, measured, routed, and settled in real time.

The creator economy is where the system proves itself first — but the architecture is built for a broader world where humans and agents create continuously across every surface of the internet.

## How Value Flows

Every dollar flowing through the protocol follows the same cascade: **10% ARP** (Audience Reward Pool) off the top to verified audience, then a **50/50 split** between creator and platform, with the platform share allocated to treasury (70%), $555 buyback-and-burn (20%), and reserves (10%).
