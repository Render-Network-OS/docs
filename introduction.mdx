**Render Network Protocol** is a blockchain protocol on Solana that verifies audience attention and routes economic value to the people who create it. Attention is the asset, participation is rewarded, and experience is liquid across platforms.

## How Value Flows

Every dollar flowing through the protocol follows the same cascade: **10% ARP** (Audience Reward Pool) off the top to verified audience, then a **50/50 split** between creator and platform, with the platform share allocated to treasury (70%), $555 buyback-and-burn (20%), and reserves (10%).
