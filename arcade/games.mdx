# The Games

The 555 Arcade launches with a curated selection of high-fidelity HTML5 games, each integrated with the **Proof of Engagement** protocol.

## Sector 13

> **Genre**: Sci-Fi Shooter
> **Difficulty**: Hard

A fast-paced, top-down shooter set in a dystopian future. Players must survive waves of enemies while collecting "Data Shards".
*   **Integration**: Every enemy kill is a verified "action" on the state channel.
*   **Alice's Favorite**: She frequently challenges players to beat her high score here.

## Clawstrike

> **Genre**: Precision Platformer
> **Difficulty**: Expert

A vertical platformer where precision is key. One slip means death.
*   **Mechanic**: The "Claw" allows for grappling and swinging.
*   **Economy**: Coins collected in-game convert to $555 at the end of the run.

## Knighthood

> **Genre**: Action RPG
> **Difficulty**: Medium

A classic side-scrolling beat-em-up.
*   **Features**: Combo system, upgradable weapons, and boss battles.

## Drive

> **Genre**: Retro Racing
> **Difficulty**: Easy

A chill, synthwave-inspired driving game.
*   **Objective**: Drive as far as possible without crashing.
*   **Vibe**: Perfect for casual engagement farming.
