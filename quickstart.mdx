## Get started in three steps

Transform your streaming career with blockchain-powered tools and sustainable monetization.

### Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="wallet" title="Set up crypto wallet">
    Connect your preferred crypto wallet to start earning RNDR tokens and access premium streaming features.

    **Supported Wallets:**
    - Phantom (Solana)
    - MetaMask (Ethereum)
    - Solflare
    - Trust Wallet

    <Tip>RNDR tokens power all gamification and monetization features.</Tip>

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="key" title="Lottery prerequisites (Endless Raffle)">
    To participate in the Endless Raffle, ensure you meet the $555 requirement.

    **Minimum Holding:** 5,555 $555 (SPL) in your connected Solana wallet.

    <Note>
      Learn more: [$555 Token](/tokenomics/555-token) · [CTRL Control Panel](/products/ctrl-control-panel)
    </Note>

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="gamepad" title="Set up 555 Browser Source">
    Add the 555 gamification overlay to your stream using OBS browser source.

    <ul>
      <li>In OBS, add a "Browser Source"</li>
      <li>Set URL to: https://555.rendernet.work/overlay</li>
      <li>Set dimensions to 1920x1080</li>
      <li>Enable "Shutdown source when not visible"</li>
      <li>Connect your wallet when prompted</li>
    </ul>

    <Tip>Your first prediction market launches automatically after setup.</Tip>

  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 2: Configure Your Stream

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="settings" title="Set up OBS/Streamlabs">
    Integrate Render Network tools with your streaming software for seamless gamification.

    **OBS Studio Setup:**
    <ul>
      <li>Add Browser Source for 555 overlay</li>
      <li>Configure wallet connection</li>
      <li>Set up prediction market displays</li>
    </ul>

    <Tip>Use the 555 extension for automatic overlay positioning.</Tip>

</Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="sliders" title="Configure CTRL Control Panel">
    Use the web dashboard to manage themes, overlays, alerts, badges, and token-gated actions.

    **Steps:**
    - Open CTRL Control Panel and connect your Solana wallet
    - Select your stream and load or create a theme
    - Configure token-gated actions with $555 thresholds as needed
    - Publish and verify overlays in your scene

    <Note>
      See: [CTRL Control Panel](/products/ctrl-control-panel)
    </Note>

  </Accordion>
<Accordion icon="gamepad" title="Create Your First Game">
    Launch your first interactive stream with blockchain rewards.

    <ul>
      <li>Choose game type (Prediction, Trivia, Mini-games)</li>
      <li>Set RNDR token entry fees (or configure $555-based gating for eligible experiences)</li>
      <li>Configure prize distribution</li>
      <li>Go live and watch engagement soar</li>
    </ul>

    <Tip>Start with small bets to build community trust.</Tip>

  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
