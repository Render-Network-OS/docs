## Get started in four steps

Transform your streaming career with browser-based tools, blockchain-powered engagement, and sustainable monetization.

### Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="wallet" title="Set up a Solana wallet">
    Connect a Solana wallet to access $555 features and creator economies.

    **Supported Wallets:**
    - Phantom (recommended)
    - Solflare
    - Trust Wallet

    <Tip>$555 powers access, staking, and the ARP / Buybacks / Treasury engine. Learn more: [$555 Token](/tokenomics/555-token)</Tip>

  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 2: Open 555stream

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="tower-broadcast" title="Launch the browser-based studio">
    555stream is a browser-based streaming studio — no download required. Open it at [stream.rndrntwrk.com](https://stream.rndrntwrk.com) and connect your wallet.

    **What you get:**
    - 7-platform simulcast (Twitch, YouTube, Kick, X, TikTok, Zora, pump.fun)
    - Cloud encoding — no GPU load on your machine
    - Scene composition, overlays, and guest management in the browser
    - Alice (AI operator) for automated ad placement and stream management

    <Tip>No OBS or desktop software needed. Everything runs in your browser.</Tip>

  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 3: Go Live

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="play-circle" title="Configure and start your broadcast">
    Set up your stream destinations and start broadcasting:

    - Add your RTMP keys for each platform (Twitch, YouTube, Kick, etc.)
    - Choose a scene layout (Solo, 2-grid, 4-grid, PIP)
    - Configure overlays, lower thirds, and alerts
    - Hit **Start Stream** — cloud encoding handles the rest

    <Tip>Alice monitors engagement signals and triggers L-Bar ads at optimal moments automatically.</Tip>

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="gamepad" title="Add games and engagement">
    Launch interactive features to drive viewer participation:

    - Browse the [555 Arcade](/arcade/overview) — 20 games with verified engagement
    - Enable prediction markets, trivia, and mini-games
    - Configure prize distribution and entry conditions

    <Tip>Start with open-entry games to build community engagement before adding token-gated features.</Tip>

  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 4: Start Earning

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="dollar-sign" title="Activate monetization">
    Revenue flows through the standard economic cascade:

    1. **10% ARP** — Audience Reward Pool distributed to engaged viewers
    2. **Post-ARP split** — 50% creator, 50% platform
    3. **Platform share** — 70% treasury, 20% buyback-and-burn, 5% $555 reserve, 5% SOL/USDC reserve

    **To activate:**
    - Connect the Monetization Contract and campaign sources
    - Verify the ARP / Buybacks / Treasury split
    - Track earnings in real-time from the studio dashboard

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="rocket" title="Launch a Creator Token (optional)">
    Mint your own Creator Token via [Render Expand](/products/render-expand) to unlock:

    - Staker-funded revenue sharing
    - Token-gated chat, overlays, and actions
    - Buyback-and-burn mechanics for your token
    - Your own economic flywheel alongside $555

    <Note>See: [Creator Tokens](/tokenomics/creator-tokens) · [Fee Distribution](/tokenomics/fee-distribution)</Note>

  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
