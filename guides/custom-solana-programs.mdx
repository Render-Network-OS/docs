## Overview

Render Network leverages custom Solana programs (Anchor) for lottery automation, fee distribution, and randomness integrations.

## Architecture

- **Anchor Framework:** Accounts, PDAs, and instruction handlers
- **Modules:**
  - Lottery: entry, settlement, payouts
  - Fees: accrual, buybacks, burns, ecosystem allocations
  - Randomness: Switchboard VRF integration

## Integration Points

- **Events → CTRL/SDK:** Program events surface to dashboards and overlays
- **Governance/Upgrades:** Community-driven policy changes and controlled upgrades where applicable

## Transparency

- On-chain proofs, explorer links, and public dashboards
