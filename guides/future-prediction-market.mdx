## Concept

Streamers and viewers can create live yes/no or multi-outcome markets. Odds update via AMMs with SOL/USDC/SPL token support.

## Oracle Resolution

- Hybrid oracles (Pyth/Switchboard) + community voting for edge cases
- On-chain dispute and resolution timelines

## Safeguards

- Bet caps, reputation scoring, and layered anti-manipulation
- Regional access and compliance gates
