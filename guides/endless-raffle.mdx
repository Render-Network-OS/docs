## Overview

Blockchain-based raffle with transparent fairness and token-gated entry.

<Info>
  **Entry:** Comment 5 lucky numbers + hold 5,555 $555 in your connected wallet.
</Info>

## Flow

1. Entry window opens; users submit 5 numbers
2. Eligibility check verifies 5,555 $555 minimum
3. Draw uses Switchboard VRF; proof published on-chain
4. Winners paid out per configured distribution; rollover rules apply

## Randomness (VRF)

- Verifiable randomness via Switchboard
- On-chain proofs and transaction links
- Public verification dashboard (where available)

## Fees & Rollover

- 20% allocation reserved for buybacks/burns
- Rollover mechanics for unclaimed or carry-over pools

## UI States

- Open for entries → Drawing → Results Published → Payouts
