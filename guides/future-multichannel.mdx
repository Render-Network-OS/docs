## Concept

One broadcast powers multiple events without per-event token deployment. Non-deploying slots enable creators to aggregate across daily events with $555-based controls.

## Architecture Sketch

- Fan-out routing from a single ingest
- Per-event overlay layers and permissions
- Accounting aggregation across events

## Benefits

- Lower operational overhead
- Unified analytics across events
- Crypto-native controls without multi-coin complexity
