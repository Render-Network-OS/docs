# 555stream

**555stream** is a browser-based live streaming studio that lets creators broadcast to 7 platforms simultaneously without downloading anything. Cloud encoding via Cloudflare means your machine never touches the heavy lifting.

## Features

### 7-Platform Simulcast
Broadcast to all of these at the same time:
*   Twitch
*   YouTube
*   Kick
*   X (Twitter)
*   TikTok
*   Zora
*   pump.fun

One stream, seven audiences. No extra hardware, no split setups.

### Cloud Encoding
Video encoding is offloaded to Cloudflare's edge network. Your browser captures and sends; the cloud does the rest. This means:
*   No GPU load on your machine.
*   Consistent quality regardless of local hardware.
*   Lower latency through edge-node proximity.

### Scene Control
Full scene composition in the browser:
*   Templates: Solo, 2-grid, 4-grid, PIP (Picture-in-Picture).
*   Runtime controls: Lower thirds, tickers, alerts.
*   Overlay management for branding and sponsor assets.

### Guest Management
WebRTC-based guest system:
*   Invite guests to join your stream live.
*   Control guest audio/video independently.
*   Grid layouts adjust automatically.

## L-Bar Ads

The **L-Bar** is a non-interruptive ad format inspired by broadcast TV (think ESPN during live sports). Instead of cutting to an ad break, the stream content squeezes into an L-shaped region while the ad occupies the remaining space.

### How It Works
*   **No Interruption**: Content keeps playing. The viewer never loses the stream.
*   **AI-Triggered Placement**: Alice polls engagement signals every 5 seconds across 4 metrics. When the composite score crosses the 0.70 threshold, she triggers an ad placement at the optimal moment.
*   **Two-Sided Marketplace**: Advertisers create campaigns with budgets and targeting. Streamers browse and accept campaigns. The protocol handles matching and payment.

### Ad Revenue Flow
Ad revenue follows the standard economic cascade:
1.  10% ARP to audience.
2.  Post-ARP: 50% creator, 50% platform.
3.  Platform share: 70% treasury, 20% burns, 5% $555 reserve, 5% SOL/USDC reserve.

## Alice as Operator

Alice (the autonomous AI agent) can operate 555stream without human intervention:
*   **Start/Stop Broadcasts**: Alice can initiate and end streams on schedule.
*   **Trigger Ads**: Monitors engagement and places L-Bar ads at optimal moments.
*   **Manage Guests**: Invites and controls guest appearances.
*   **Control Overlays**: Switches scenes, updates tickers, manages alerts.

This enables 24/7 streaming capabilities where Alice runs the broadcast and the creator joins for structured segments.

## Architecture

555stream runs on a multi-layer stack:

| Layer | Role |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Browser Client** | Real-time video capture and WebRTC communication |
| **Edge Encoding** | Cloud-based video encoding and media storage |
| **Control Plane** | Scene management, source routing, and configuration |
| **SFU Service** | Selective Forwarding Unit for multi-party WebRTC |
| **Media Engine** | Video composition and transcoding pipeline |
| **Capture Service** | Browser-based capture for recording and clips |
