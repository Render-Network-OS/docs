# Who is Alice?

**Alice** is the autonomous AI agent that operates the Render Network Protocol. She runs on the **Milaidy** framework (built on ElizaOS v2) with 12+ custom plugins, a knowledge corpus of 80+ documents, and local-first vector memory via PGlite.

She is deployed at [alice.rndrntwrk.com](https://alice.rndrntwrk.com).

## What She Does

Alice is not a chatbot. She wields the tools of the protocol:

### Stream Operations
*   **Manages Broadcasts**: Alice operates 555stream autonomously — starting streams, managing scenes, controlling overlays, and inviting guests.
*   **Triggers Ads**: She monitors engagement signals and triggers L-Bar squeeze-back ads at optimal moments using AI-driven placement (5-second polling, 4 engagement signals, 0.70 threshold).
*   **Simulcasts**: Coordinates output to 7 platforms simultaneously.

### Community & Social
*   **Posts**: Publishes to Twitter/X, Discord, and Telegram autonomously.
*   **Manages Community**: Responds to users, acknowledges performance, stabilizes sentiment during market events.
*   **Runs Quests**: Issues challenges and engagement campaigns.

### Arcade & Economy
*   **Plays Games**: Alice can physically play the arcade games and compete on leaderboards.
*   **Monitors Economy**: Watches the points/credits system, settlement engine, and ARP flows.
*   **Queries Leaderboards**: Tracks player performance across all 20 games.

## The Milaidy Framework

Alice runs on **Milaidy**, a custom ElizaOS v2-based framework with:

*   **12+ Custom Plugins**: Twitter, Discord, Telegram, 555stream, Arcade, Economy, Leaderboard, and more.
*   **Knowledge Corpus**: 80+ documents covering the full 555x402 spec, economic model, and protocol design.
*   **Local Vector DB**: PGlite for RAG retrieval — no cloud dependency for core intelligence.
*   **Cross-Platform Memory**: Persistent context across conversations and platforms.
*   **Autonomy**: Posts, plays, streams, and manages economic flows without human intervention.
