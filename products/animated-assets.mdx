## Professional Animation Library

A library of programmable animated assets designed for live economic media on RNDRNTWRK. Transitions, interactive elements that react to on-chain events, and broadcast-grade motion -- all integrated into the network's visual surfaces.

## What You Can Build

- Overlays: lower‑thirds, sidebars, banners, carousels, full‑screen takeovers
- Reactions: emotes, spark trails, confetti, screen shake, chat highlights
- Game FX: countdowns, reveal animations, win/loss flourishes, scoreboards
- Billboard Animations: branded intros/outros for sponsors and segments
- Clips & Reveal Frames: animated frames and stingers for highlight clips

## Sources & Creation

- Curated Packs: pre‑built sets from creators and community artists
- Stream Pack Generator: generate custom packs that match your brand style
- Auto Pilot: one‑click recommendations and generation based on your theme
- Token‑Gated Packs: unlock premium packs by holding/staking $555 or a Creator Token

## Integration & Triggers (CTRL Panel)

Tie animations to events across the OS:

- Alerts: follows, subs, tips, milestones
- Games: prediction resolutions, raid/quest completions, mini‑game wins
- Billboard: ad start/stop, sponsored segments, product placements
- Chat & Commands: reactions on keywords, mod triggers, timed effects

## Performance & Delivery

- Formats: WebGL/Canvas, Lottie JSON, sprite sheets, video fallback
- Adaptive quality and size budgets; preloading and caching for smooth playback
- Safe defaults for 1920×1080 with responsive presets for mobile and vertical

## Licensing & Marketplace

- Creator and artist revenue share on pack sales
- Simple licenses for stream usage; attribution optional where allowed
- Versioned updates with change logs and rollback support

## Creator Workflow

1) Open CTRL Panel → Animated Assets
2) Browse packs or generate with Stream Pack Generator
3) Preview, adjust theme variables (colors, fonts, timing), and assign triggers
4) Publish and verify in your overlay (Browser Source 1920×1080)
5) Optionally schedule via Auto Pilot
