## Why Collectives

Independent creators gain negotiating power by aggregating metrics and inventory. Collectives present standardized, verifiable packages to advertisers without sacrificing creator independence.

## Mechanics

- Shared Profile: aggregate engagement, reach, and staking TVL
- Shared Offers: standardized placement specs across members
- Shared Payouts: PDA-based distribution trees with automatic splits

## Governance

- Admission/removal proposals
- Shared policy bounds (min buyback %, split ranges)
- Treasury and operations budget (if applicable)
