## Overview

CTRL is the web-based dashboard for configuring your stream’s look-and-feel and interactive controls—without external software. Manage themes, overlays, alerts, badges, and token-gated actions directly from your browser.

<Info>
  Access to advanced features can be token-gated with $555. Connect your Solana
  wallet to unlock creator tools and per-stream controls.
</Info>

## Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Themes & Overlays" icon="layout">
    Create, edit, preview, and publish visual themes and overlay packs.
    Export/import configurations to reuse across streams.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Alerts & Badges" icon="bell">
    Configure real-time alerts (follows, tips) and badge systems for loyal
    viewers. Tie triggers to on-chain events.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Token Connect & Gating" icon="wallet">
    Connect your wallet and gate specific controls (e.g., overlay triggers, chat
    modes) behind token thresholds using $555.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Per-Stream Configuration" icon="sliders">
    Maintain per-stream settings including token verification rules, moderation
    defaults, and overlay scene layouts.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Access & Permissions

- **Wallet Ownership:** The connected wallet defines stream ownership and access to gated modules.
- **Moderator Roles:** Grant additional wallets scoped permissions (e.g., manage overlays, run raffles, moderate chat).
- **Per-Stream Rules:** Use per-stream token verification to enforce minimum holdings for interaction.
