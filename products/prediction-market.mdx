## Overview

Create live yes/no or multi-outcome markets during your stream. Odds update via AMM mechanics and resolve with hybrid oracles (Pyth/Switchboard) plus optional community voting.

<Info>
  Integrated with CTRL for display and controls. Token-gated interactions can be
  configured via $555 thresholds.
</Info>
