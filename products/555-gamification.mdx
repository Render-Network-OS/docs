## Overview

555 is the gamification engine that transforms passive viewers into engaged participants using blockchain rewards. It's not just donations - it's an entire gaming ecosystem built around your stream.

<Info>
  555 is currently LIVE in stealth mode. This is the first product shipping from
  Render Network.
</Info>

## Core Features

### Interactive Games

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Prediction Markets" icon="trending-up">
    Let viewers bet on stream outcomes using RNDR tokens:
    - Game results and match outcomes
    - Stream milestones and achievements
    - Challenge completions and personal bests
    - Real-world events and predictions

    **Revenue Split**: 70% to streamer, 30% prize pool, 2% house edge

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Mini-Games" icon="gamepad">
    Integrated games viewers can play during streams:
    - Chat-based RPG elements and quests
    - Trivia challenges with RNDR rewards
    - Reaction time and speed challenges
    - Memory and pattern matching games

    **Prize Distribution**: 50% to streamer, 30% to winners, 20% to network

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Achievement System" icon="trophy">
    Progressive viewer engagement mechanics:
    - Loyalty points and 555 Points accumulation
    - Badge collections and rarity tiers
    - Leaderboards and seasonal competitions
    - Exclusive NFT rewards for top performers

    **Rewards**: Custom stream overlays, priority features, RNDR bonuses

  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
