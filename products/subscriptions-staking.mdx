## Concept

Subscriptions become investments. Viewers stake into a creator’s economy to unlock benefits and share in revenue distributed via the 70% Profit Pool.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect & Stake">
    Viewers connect their wallet and stake to the creator’s economy. Staking may use $555 or the Creator Token depending on the creator’s configuration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn & Participate">
    Stakeholders accrue rewards from the Profit Pool and gain voting power for the creator/stakeholder split within bounds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Unstake & Cooldown">
    Unstaking is subject to per‑creator cooldowns to preserve stability.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Benefits

- Priority access to features and drops
- Profit sharing via the algorithmic split
- Voting rights for split adjustments and policy bounds
