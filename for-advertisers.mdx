- **Two-sided marketplace** — browse creators and collectives, target by verified metrics

## The L-Bar Ad Format

The L-Bar is a non-interruptive ad unit. Instead of cutting to an ad break, the stream content squeezes into an L-shaped region while the ad occupies the remaining space. The viewer never loses the stream.

**How placement works:**
- Alice polls 4 engagement signals every 5 seconds
- When the composite engagement score crosses the **0.70 threshold**, she triggers an ad placement
- Metrics driving placement: watch time, active plays, engagement score, staking TVL
- Result: Ads appear at moments of peak attention, not during lulls

## Campaign Options

### Option A: Pay for Ads

Direct budget injection into the protocol's distribution system:

- **Pricing models**: CPM, CPC, or CPA
- **Bidding**: Fixed price or auction with reserve floors and auto-bid ranges
- **Pacing**: Total and daily budgets with even or as-fast-as-possible delivery
- **Scheduling**: Flight windows, dayparting, frequency caps per viewer or per session
- **Creative**: Weighting and A/B variants supported

## Revenue Flow (Full Transparency)

When you fund a campaign, every dollar is traceable on Solana:

| Step | Allocation | Recipient |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. ARP** | 10% off the top | Directly to the audience you reached (verified engagement) |
| **2. Creator** | 50% of post-ARP | Creator's wallet |
| **3. Platform** | 50% of post-ARP | 70% treasury, 20% $555 buyback-and-burn, 5% $555 reserve, 5% SOL/USDC reserve |

Programmatic payouts via PDAs with receipt events. No manual intervention, no hidden fees.
