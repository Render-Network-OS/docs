## Distribution

### No VC. No Seed. No Private Sale.
$555 was distributed with a simple principle: the community gets the overwhelming majority.

*   **92% Public**: Available through open market distribution.
*   **8% Team**: Locked for 5 years. No early unlock, no accelerated vesting.

## Utility

### Staking (VAP v2)
*   Minimum stake of 55,555 $555 tokens to participate in Proof of Engagement.
*   Stake weight follows logarithmic formula: `ln(1 + S/27,778)` capped at 5.0.
*   7-day activation period, 90-day maturation to full weight.

### Structural Burns
*   20% of platform revenue buys $555 on the open market and burns it permanently.
*   Every dollar of ecosystem activity removes tokens from circulation.
*   Burns are protocol-enforced — not discretionary.

### Attention Credits (VAP v2)
*   Burn $555 at oracle price to mint Attention Credits (1 AC = $0.001 USDC).
*   ACs are the bidding unit in epoch auctions for verified airtime.

### Creator Coins
*   Creators can launch coins through pump.fun with enforced splits:
    *   1% fee on trades
    *   10% to Audience Reward Pool
    *   20% to buybacks
    *   Remainder to creator

### Governance
*   ve$555 DAO for protocol parameter governance.
*   Token-weighted voting on epoch parameters, fee schedules, and protocol upgrades.

## Deflationary Mechanics

The token is designed for structural deflation through multiple burn channels:

1.  **Platform Burns**: 20% of platform revenue → buy and burn.
2.  **Attention Credit Burns**: $555 burned to mint ACs for epoch auctions.
3.  **Trading Fees**: 0.5% fee on every trade, portion allocated to burns.

There is no minting function. Supply can only decrease.
